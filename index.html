Humanitarian Foundation

Queen Bridget is a lawyer by profession. She is passionate about humanity and humanitarian activities. She is the founder of a non-profit and non-governmental organization. A foundation that, advocates for human rights and women rights. Queen Bridget Foundation was born out of passion and love to help the needy, and empower women through awareness of health issues and women's rights. Today the foundation now focuses on child education and is committed and dedicated to empowering the girl-child through scholarship, awareness sessions and advocacy on women's rights, sexual rights, child marriage, child hawking, VVF, menstrual health, and hygiene, and challenging the stereotypes and societal norms against the girl-child.
