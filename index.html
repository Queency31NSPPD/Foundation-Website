Queen Bridget Foundation

Humanitarian Foundation

Queen Bridget is a lawyer by profession. She is passionate about humanity and humanitarian activities. She is the founder of a non-profit and non-governmental organization. A foundation that, advocates for human rights and women rights, creates awareness through education and is committed to changing the narrative and stereotype about women and especially the girl-child, specifically on women's rights, sexual rights, girl-child marriage, girl-child hawking, VVF, menstrual health, and hygiene. Queen Bridget Foundation was born out of passion and love to help the needy, and empower women through awareness of health issues and women's rights. Today the foundation now focuses on the girl child and is committed to empowering the girl-child through awareness sessions and education, challenging the stereotypes and societal norms against the girl-child.
